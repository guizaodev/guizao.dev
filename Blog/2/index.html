PS5 Reveal Event Review

Sony held an event today revealing tons of information regarding the upcoming PS5 and the games + hardware you can get with the console, this is everything coming from Sony in the future regarding games and hardware!

Hardware

Todays reveal held a lot of suprising, one of those suprises was all of the hardware for the PS5.

  • PULSE 3D™ wireless headset – offering 3D audio support and dual noise-cancelling microphones
  • HD Camera – featuring dual 1080p lenses for gamers to broadcast themselves along with their epic gameplay moments
  • Media Remote – a remote control with built-in microphone to navigate movies and streaming services with ease
  • DualSense™ Charging Station – for convenient charging of two DualSense™ Wireless Controllers.
  • PlayStation 5 Normal & Digital Edition Console – Different types for different types of gamers.

There has yet to be a answer to the long asked question of "How much will the Playstation 5 console cost?" but people think it will be anywhere from $499-699 USD.

Final Verdict

In my personal opinion, Sony knocked it out of the park with this event, and while they didn't reveal the console itself until the very end they showcased some really cool games and i'm excited for the future of the console. Now regarding if I will buy the console all depends on the pricing of it and the exclusives it offers. I'm more excited for the games themselves, and the simple fact that games I enjoyed growing up such as Little Big Planet are still having new games apart of the same franchises being developed.
